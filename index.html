Believe with Suresh Raina

Bharat sundharesan

In "Believe," Suresh Raina shares his inspiring journey as a young cricketer, marked by perseverance and resilience. Adversity struck early, with bullying in school and at cricket camps, but Raina's unwavering spirit led him to rise above every challenge. With the word "Believe" tattooed on his arm, he embraces the profound lessons learned from cricket legends like M.S. Dhoni, Sachin Tendulkar, and Rahul Dravid.
